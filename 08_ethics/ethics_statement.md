# 08 · Ethical Statement

## Ethical Principles

This doctoral research follows the ethical principles established by the Universidad Nacional Mayor de San Marcos.

---

## Participant Rights

Participants will receive complete information regarding the objectives of the study before providing informed consent.

---

## Confidentiality

Personal information collected during the research will remain confidential and will only be used for academic purposes.

---

## Data Protection

Research data will be securely stored and protected against unauthorized access.

---

## Artificial Intelligence Ethics

The Artificial Intelligence components developed during this project will promote transparency, fairness, accountability, and responsible use of technology.
