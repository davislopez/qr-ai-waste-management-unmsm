# 07 · Model Card

## Model Name

QR-AI Waste Management Information System

---

## Purpose

To improve municipal solid waste management through an intelligent information system that integrates QR Code technology and Artificial Intelligence.

---

## Intended Users

- Municipal governments
- Environmental managers
- Waste management operators
- Researchers

---

## Input Data

- Household identification
- QR Code records
- Waste collection information
- Citizen participation records

---

## Output

The system generates:

- Participation indicators
- Waste management reports
- Operational statistics
- Decision-support information

---

## Benefits

- Better monitoring
- Digital traceability
- Faster information processing
- Improved municipal decision-making

---

## Limitations

The Artificial Intelligence module will be progressively developed and validated during the doctoral research.
