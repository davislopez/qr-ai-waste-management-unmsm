# 11 · Research Integrity

## Purpose

This document describes the principles of scientific integrity that guide the development of this doctoral research.

---

## Research Integrity Principles

This research follows internationally accepted principles of responsible research conduct:

- Honesty in reporting results
- Transparency in research methods
- Reproducibility of scientific findings
- Accountability throughout the research process
- Objectivity during data collection and analysis

---

## Good Scientific Practices

The project follows these practices:

- Proper citation of all information sources
- Ethical management of research data
- Version control using GitHub
- Continuous documentation of research progress
- Compliance with institutional research policies

---

## Responsible Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence will be used to support:

- Data preprocessing
- Pattern recognition
- Statistical analysis
- Decision support

All AI-generated results will be reviewed and validated by the researcher before interpretation.

---

## Data Integrity

The project guarantees:

- Secure data storage
- Data confidentiality
- Controlled access
- Backup procedures
- Data consistency verification

---

## Reproducibility

To improve research reproducibility, the repository includes:

- Research protocol
- Literature review
- Methodology documentation
- Research workflow
- Bias assessment
- Data management plan

Future versions will include source code and implementation details.

---

## Expected Contribution

The repository promotes transparent, reproducible, and responsible doctoral research following international standards.
