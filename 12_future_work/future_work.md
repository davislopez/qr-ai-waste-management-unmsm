# 12 · Future Work

## Future Research Directions

This doctoral research will continue with the following development phases:

1. Development of the QR Code-based Information System.
2. Artificial Intelligence integration for municipal decision support.
3. Machine Learning model development for waste generation prediction.
4. Mobile application development for households and municipal staff.
5. Cloud database implementation.
6. Pilot implementation in Amarilis District, Huánuco.
7. Statistical validation of research hypotheses.
8. Publication of scientific papers in indexed journals.
9. Presentation of results at international conferences.
10. Development of an open-source decision-support platform.

---

## Long-Term Vision

The long-term objective is to build an intelligent environmental management platform capable of supporting municipalities in Peru through digital transformation, Artificial Intelligence, and data-driven decision-making.
