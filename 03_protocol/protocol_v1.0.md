# Protocol v1.0

## Project Title

**Effect of Implementing a Quick Response (QR) Code-Based Information System on Improving Solid Waste Management Among Households Participating in the Source Segregation Program in Amarilis District, Huánuco, Peru – 2026**

## Research Line

Environmental Management and Sustainable Development

## Research Subline

Digital Transformation and Intelligent Systems for Environmental Management

## Research Objective

To evaluate the effect of implementing a QR Code-based information system on improving municipal solid waste management among households participating in the source segregation program in Amarilis District, Huánuco, Peru.

## Methodology

- Quantitative research
- Applied research
- Quasi-experimental design
- QR Code-based Information System
- Artificial Intelligence
- Statistical data analysis

## Ethical Considerations

The study will ensure participant confidentiality, informed consent, secure data management, and compliance with research ethics principles.

## Timeline

The project will be conducted during 2026 as part of the Doctoral Program in Deep Tech with a Focus on Artificial Intelligence and Emerging Technologies at Universidad Nacional Mayor de San Marcos.
