# 10 · Bias Audit

## Objective

To identify, evaluate, and mitigate potential sources of bias throughout the doctoral research.

---

## Potential Sources of Bias

| Source | Description | Mitigation |
|---------|-------------|------------|
| Sampling Bias | Households may not fully represent the district population. | Random selection whenever possible. |
| Observer Bias | Researchers may influence observations. | Standardized observation protocols. |
| Response Bias | Participants may provide socially desirable answers. | Anonymous questionnaires. |
| Technology Bias | QR adoption depends on smartphone availability. | Alternative participation methods. |
| Algorithmic Bias | AI models may favor certain patterns. | Model validation and performance evaluation. |

---

## Evaluation Strategy

- Internal validity assessment
- External validity assessment
- Cross-validation of AI models
- Statistical significance testing

---

## Expected Outcome

A transparent evaluation of possible biases and documentation of mitigation strategies.
