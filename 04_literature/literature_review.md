# 04 · Literature Review

## Introduction

Municipal solid waste management has become a major environmental challenge worldwide. Recent advances in Artificial Intelligence, Information Systems, and Digital Transformation provide new opportunities to improve monitoring, operational efficiency, and evidence-based decision-making.

---

# Research Topics

## Smart Waste Management

Smart waste management integrates digital technologies to optimize waste collection, monitoring, and recycling processes.

## QR Code Technology

QR Codes provide low-cost identification mechanisms that improve traceability, citizen participation, and information management.

## Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence supports prediction, pattern recognition, and intelligent decision-making using historical and real-time data.

## Environmental Information Systems

Information Systems improve municipal management by integrating data collection, processing, visualization, and reporting.

---

# Research Gap

Most previous studies analyze QR Code systems or Artificial Intelligence independently.

Few studies integrate both technologies to improve municipal solid waste management in developing countries, particularly in Peru.

---

# Scientific Contribution

- Artificial Intelligence
